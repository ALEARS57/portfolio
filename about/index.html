My Work

Design has always been my way of understanding the world — not just how things look, but why they work the way they do. I didn’t follow a straight path into this field; I followed instincts, tutorials, and the occasional design rabbit hole until it all started to make sense.

What I enjoy most about design is that it sits right at the intersection of creativity and logic. I love finding that balance — the part where I get to shape something beautiful and make sure it actually works. That’s also why I care deeply about development: I don’t just want to hand off files — I want to understand what’s possible, push boundaries, and sometimes build it myself.

Whether I’m prototyping a UI, testing out interactions, or refining layouts for clarity and emotion, my goal stays the same: to create experiences that feel thoughtful, intuitive, and quietly delightful.

This portfolio is a snapshot of that mindset — visual storytelling backed by intention and a little bit of code. 😊
